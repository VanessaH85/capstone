About

The Potheads are an English girl group formed in 1998, consisting of Hermione Granger a.k.a. Brainy ; Ginny Weasley aka Sporty; and Luna Lovegood aka Whimsy. With their "harry potter power" mantra, they redefined the girl-group concept by targeting a young female fanbase. They led the teen pop resurgence of the 1990s and 2000s, were a major part of the Cool Britannia era, and became pop culture icons of the decades.
